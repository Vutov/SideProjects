About Us

Our mission: To publish novels that honor Stargate SG-1 and Stargate Atlantis and their fans.

If you haven't figured it out already, we are fans of these shows. We are also professional writers, artists and publishers.

We are not interested in turning out books as "merchandise". We love Stargate and are honored that we have this opportunity to share our enthusiasm with fellow fans.

Then everyone involved - from the author and editor to the cover artist and publisher - are fans of the show; when they’re all involved with the project because they love Stargate and want to be part of something really special; and when they’re all determined to make every detail about every book as good as it can possibly be, then we think you’ll have a product worthy of the fans and the show that has inspired us all.
